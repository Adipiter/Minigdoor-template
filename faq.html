GENERAL QUESTION

What is MiningDoor?

MiningDoor is a platform providing with computing power sharing services to users around the world. It saves users from the complicated process of purchasing, installing, and hosting miners. You can enjoy the service with just one-click.

How MiningDoor Works？
On the platform, Once the payment is confirmed, MiningDoor will supply mining power to the pool and the pool will directly transfer the revenue to the customer’s MiningDoor account. MiningDoor will be responsible for providing mining power to the mining pools per customers’ demand, meaning they are not required personally purchase miners. MiningDoor has the largest mining pool in the world and the most advanced miner management team. It promises to provide its customers with a secure and consistent computing power sharing service.
How do I open my MiningDoor Account?
It's quite easy and convenient. Follow this link, fill in the registration form and then press "Register".
How does the cloud mining earning scheme work?
All is clear. The investor should determine the optimum capacity for himself, buy a cloud mining contract, and earn income every day depending on the parameters of the chosen contract.
How can I withdraw funds?
Login to your account using your username and password and check the Withdraw section.
What is the minimum withdrawable amount from the account?
The minimum withdrawable amount is 0.0001 BTC.
What is the fees for withdrawing funds?
5.99%
After I make a withdrawal request, when will the funds be available on my ecurrency account?
Withdrawal process time is from instant to 24 hours.
How do you calculate the interest on my account?

Interest on your MiningDoor - Bitcoin Cloud Mining Platform account is acquired Daily, Monthly and Yearly and credited to your available balance at the end of each day.

Can I do a direct deposit from my account balance?
Yes! To make a deposit from your MiningDoor - Bitcoin Cloud Mining Platform account balance. Simply login into your members account and click on Make Deposit ans select the Deposit from Account Balance Radio button.
Can I make an additional deposit to MiningDoor - Bitcoin Cloud Mining Platform account once it has been opened?
Yes, you can but all transactions are handled separately.
What if I can't log into my account because I forgot my password?
Click forgot password link, type your username or e-mail and you'll receive your account information.
How can I change my e-mail address or password?
Log into your MiningDoor account and click on the "Account Information". You can change your e-mail address and password there.
Can I register more than one account?
No. If we find that one member has more than one account, the entire funds will be frozen.
How exactly are the different levels of the partner program structured?
Our affiliate program is divided into 2 levels. I) When your partner does a deposit, you received a referral commission 10%. II) When your partner's partner will do a deposit, you will receive a referral commission 5%.
Where can I find my affiliate link? How can I recruit new people?
In the logged-in state under the tab "Affiliate Program" you will find your personal referral link. You can share this link with your friends or post it on social networks. You can automatically and easily share your link with the fields below your link on Facebook and Twitter.
