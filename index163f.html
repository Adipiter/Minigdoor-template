How to make a deposit?

Log into your account, pick "Make a deposit" menu, then input the deposit amount and click "Miner." Follow your payment system's instructions to confirm your deposit.

  • In your personal account click "Miner"

How to get a referral bonus

The referrer bonus is a prize given for bringing in new investors. The referral bonus is 10% & 5% of the deposit of the new investor. You must copy your personal referral link from your account and give it to your partners in order to participate in the referral program.

  • Create a new account
  • Log in to your personal account
  • You will get a referral link automatically , which can be found in your personal account
  • Invite friends using your referral link
  • Get bonus from deposit of each invited friend
